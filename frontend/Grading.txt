GRADING SYSTEM ===========================================================>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>

Rubric Name: "Essay Grading Standard"

Criteria 1: Content Quality (5 points)
- Description: How well does the answer cover the topic?
- 5 points: Excellent, comprehensive
- 3 points: Good, covers main points
- 1 point: Poor, missing key information

Criteria 2: Grammar & Structure (3 points)
- Description: Writing quality and organization
- 3 points: No errors, well-organized
- 2 points: Few errors
- 1 point: Many errors

Criteria 3: Critical Thinking (2 points)
- Description: Analysis and insight shown
- 2 points: Excellent analysis
- 1 point: Basic understanding
